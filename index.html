Test Day Bot

This bot is here to help users in #TestDay on irc.mozilla.org. Currently it doesn't do much except greet users as they enter the room as help post links!

:bug or !bug

Adding !bug or :bug to a message will cause the bot to post a link to Bug Writing Guidelines

:etherpad or !etherpad

Adding :etherpad or !etherpad to a message will cause the bot to post a link to the current etherpad
